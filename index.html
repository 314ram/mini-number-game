How to Play ->

  1. Click the "START" button
  2. Choose one of three cells (red, green, blue) in the upper section
  3. Choose the first number from the six option
  4. Choose the operator
  5. Choose the second number
  6. Repeat the process to all of three cells until its sum equals the "question cell" on the top-right
  7. "RESET" can be used to remove all the numbers you chose before in case you guessed it wrong
  8. Every number can only be chosen once
